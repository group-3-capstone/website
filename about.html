Support student learning, and get a tasty meal while you're at it.

Inspired by John Ware's famous 9999 cattle brand, 4Nines Diner is SAIT's primary student-run dining facility.

Our menu changes daily, offering quick service and slow-cooked meals, as well as pastries and beverages. All sales go towards supporting the Hospitality and Tourism program here at SAIT.
